The Kings of the Wild

Explore the world of the five largest big cats

The Mighty Lion

Lions are known as the kings of the jungle and live in prides.

The Stealthy Tiger

Tigers are the largest big cats and are solitary hunters.

The Jungle Warrior

Jaguars have the strongest bite force of any big cat.

The Master Climber

Leopards are excellent climbers and can carry prey up trees.

The Speed Demon

Cheetahs are the fastest land animals, reaching speeds up to 75 mph.
